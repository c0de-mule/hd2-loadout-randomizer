<BUILT ON BREAK>

I kept running the same loadouts every game. Same weapons, same stratagems, same comfort picks. I wanted to break out of that but just picking random stuff yourself doesn't really work. You either end up with something completely unplayable or you chicken out and grab your usual kit anyway.

So I built something that forces you out of your comfort zone without setting you up to fail. Every stratagem is scored for anti-tank and crowd clear, so Mission Ready mode always gives you something viable. Just not what you'd normally pick. Squad mode makes sure your whole team covers each other without any overlap.

I put this together over a couple weeks after work, started running it with my friends, and figured other people might get something out of it too. The Helldivers community has given me a lot and I wanted to give something back.

No ads. No tracking. No account. Just roll.
